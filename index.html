Key Stadium Events On This Day in History

On This Day

1895

The lowest attendance at Anfield is recorded at 1,000 for a match against Loughborough

1940

Liverpool is blitzed by the German Luftwaffe during the Blitzkrieg bombing campaign with Anfield suffering significant structural damage

2012

Following years of speculation and failed attempts at securing financing for stadium redevelopment, local authority approval is granted for proposed expansion of the Main Stand, increasing total capacity from 45,000 to 54,000

2019

Manager Jurgen Klopp signs a 3 year contract extension, citing a desire to remain at the club to witness the expansion of the Anfield Road Stand during his tenure
